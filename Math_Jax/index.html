$$Math~Equations$$


1. Standard Deviation

$$ \sigma~=~\sqrt{\frac{\sum fx^2}{\sum f}-\left (\frac{\sum fx}{\sum f}\right)^2} $$

2. Poission's Probability Distribution

$$ P(r)~=~\frac{e^{-m}~ m^r}{r!} $$

3. Normal Probability Distribution

$$ f(x)~=~\frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2\pi}}~e^{-\frac{1}{2}~\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^2}$$

4. Gamma Probability Distribution

$$ f(x)~=~\frac{1}{\Gamma(k)\theta^k}~x^{k-1}~e^{-\frac{x}{\theta}}$$

5. Fourier Transform

$$ f(x)~=~\frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}~f({\lambda})~e^{i{\lambda}x} dx$$

6. Laplace Transform

$$ L~\{f(x)\}~=~\int_{0}^{\infty}~e^{-sx}~f(x)~dx $$

7. Roots of Equation

$$ x~=~\frac{-b~_-^+~\sqrt{b^2 -4ac}}{2a} $$

8. Combined One-Dimensional Heat Conduction Equation

$$ \frac{1}{r^n} \frac{\delta}{\delta r} (r^2 k \frac{\delta T}{\delta r})+e_{gen}~=~\rho c \frac{\delta}{\delta t} $$

9. Fin Equation (Convection at Tip)

$$ Q_{convection}~=~\sqrt{hpkA}~(T_b - T_{\infty})~\frac{sinh (mL)+(h/mk)~cosh(mL)}{cosh (mL)+(h/mk)~sinh(mL)} $$

10. Limits

$$ f'(a)~=~\lim_{x\to0}~\frac{f(x)-f(a)}{x-a} $$
